Our Story

From a home kitchen to Johannesburg's most trusted artisan bakery.

How We Started

EMT Bakery was founded in 2015 by three lifelong friends – Esi, Mpho, and Tumi – united by a shared passion for baking and a belief that truly great bread could strengthen a community. The name “EMT” is drawn from the first initial of each founder, a constant reminder that the bakery is built on friendship, trust, and shared purpose.

What began as weekend baking sessions in a home kitchen quickly attracted attention at local Johannesburg markets. Customers kept returning – not just for the bread, but for the experience of knowing exactly who baked it and where the ingredients came from. Within eighteen months, demand outgrew the home kitchen, and the trio invested in a full commercial bakery space in the heart of Johannesburg.

Today, EMT Bakery produces over 200 individual baked items per week, serving walk-in retail customers, repeat corporate clients, and couples commissioning bespoke wedding cakes. Despite the growth, the founders remain hands-on in the kitchen every day – because for EMT Bakery, baking will always be personal.

Our Mission & Core Values

“To nourish and delight every customer through the art of traditional baking – using only the finest locally sourced ingredients, delivered with warmth, consistency, and genuine care.”

– EMT Bakery Mission Statement
Quality
We use only premium, locally sourced South African ingredients. Every recipe is tested rigorously before it reaches our shelves. We would never serve anything we would not proudly serve our own families.
Community
We partner with local farms, source from South African suppliers, and actively participate in our neighbourhood. A portion of our weekly baked surplus is donated to community food programmes in Johannesburg.
Freshness
Every single product is baked on the day it is sold or delivered. We never freeze, pre-bake, or use preservatives. If it is not fresh, it does not leave our kitchen.
Inclusivity
We believe everyone deserves great baked goods. We offer gluten-free, vegan, sugar-free, and halal-certified options, and we are always willing to accommodate special dietary requirements with advance notice.
Craftsmanship
Our bakers combine time-honoured techniques with modern innovation. Every loaf, every tier, every decoration is considered and intentional. We take enormous pride in the work that leaves our kitchen.

Meet the Bakers

Behind every exceptional loaf and every beautifully decorated cake is a dedicated team of passionate professionals.

Esi – Co-Founder & Head Baker

With over 14 years of artisan baking experience, Esi leads all bread production at EMT Bakery. She trained under a master baker in Cape Town before returning to Johannesburg to co-found EMT. Her sourdough programme is the backbone of the bakery's identity.

Mpho – Co-Founder & Lead Cake Designer

Mpho is the creative force behind every custom and wedding cake at EMT Bakery. A self-taught cake artist with a background in visual design, Mpho brings an extraordinary eye for colour, proportion, and detail to every tier she creates.

Tumi – Co-Founder & Operations Director

Tumi ensures that every order is fulfilled on time, every client is delighted, and the bakery runs like clockwork. With a background in hospitality management, she bridges the worlds of food craft and business – keeping EMT Bakery professional, reliable, and growing.
