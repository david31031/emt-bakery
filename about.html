Our Story

From a home kitchen to Johannesburg's most trusted artisan bakery.

Est. 2015

How We Started

EMT Bakery was founded in 2015 by three lifelong friends – Esi, Mpho, and Tumi – united by a shared passion for baking and a belief that truly great bread could strengthen a community. The name "EMT" is drawn from the first initial of each founder.

What began as weekend baking sessions at local Johannesburg markets quickly attracted attention. Within eighteen months, demand outgrew the home kitchen and the trio invested in a full commercial bakery in the heart of Johannesburg.

Today, EMT Bakery produces over 200 individual baked items per week, serving walk-in retail customers, repeat corporate clients, and couples commissioning bespoke wedding cakes.

Our Purpose

Mission & Core Values

“To nourish and delight every customer through the art of traditional baking – using only the finest locally sourced ingredients, delivered with warmth, consistency, and genuine care.”

– EMT Bakery Mission Statement
Quality
Only premium, locally sourced South African ingredients. Every recipe tested rigorously before reaching our shelves.
Community
We partner with local farms, source from South African suppliers, and donate surplus goods weekly to Johannesburg food programmes.
Freshness
Every product baked on the day it is sold or delivered. We never freeze, pre-bake, or use preservatives.
Inclusivity
Gluten-free, vegan, sugar-free, and halal-certified options available. Special dietary requirements accommodated with advance notice.
Craftsmanship
Time-honoured techniques meet modern innovation. Every loaf and layer is considered and intentional.
The People Behind the Bread

Meet the Bakers
