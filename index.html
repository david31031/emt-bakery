Our Commitment

Why Choose EMT Bakery?

  • 100% Locally Sourced Ingredients – We partner with South African farms for the freshest produce in every bake.
  • Dietary Options Available – Gluten-free, vegan, sugar-free, and halal-certified products available on request.
  • Same-Week Delivery – Orders placed before Wednesday delivered or ready for collection by Friday across Johannesburg.
  • Corporate & Bulk Orders Welcome – Dedicated account management and volume discounts on orders over R 1 500.
  • No Preservatives – Every product baked fresh on the day of delivery or collection. Never frozen or pre-packaged.
  • Free Design Consultations – Wedding and celebration cake clients receive a complimentary tasting and design session.
