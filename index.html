Why Choose EMT Bakery?

  • 100% Locally Sourced Ingredients – We partner with South African farms and suppliers to ensure the freshest, highest-quality produce in every bake.
  • Dietary Options Available – Gluten-free, vegan, sugar-free, and halal-certified products available. Please enquire when ordering.
  • Same-Week Delivery – Online orders placed before Wednesday are delivered or ready for collection by Friday across greater Johannesburg.
  • Corporate & Bulk Orders Welcome – Dedicated account management for regular corporate clients. Volume discounts apply on orders over R 1 500.
  • No Preservatives – Every product is baked fresh on the day of delivery or collection. We never freeze or pre-package our goods.
  • Custom Design Consultations – Wedding and celebration cake clients receive a free one-hour in-person design and tasting session.
